Anya's January Playlist

The yummiest tracks off the tastiest albums in January. These are my favorite tracks from my favorite albums this month, new and old. Feel free to substitute ingredients and prepare the dish how you'd like. Bon Appetit!

Ingredients:

  • Death Grips is Online, Death Grips
  • AAA by HYUKOH, 落日飛車 Sunset Rollercoaster
  • Post, Bjork
  • EUSEXUA, FKA twigs

Directions:

  1. Start off mellow, Listen to Aaaannnnteeeeennnaaaaaa, Track 7 off AAA.
  2. Next, lets lift the energy and listen to Hyperballad, Track 2 off Post.
  3. Lets really start getting into it. Listen to Girl Feels Good, Track 2 off EUSEXUA.
  4. Finally, end off the listening session with a bang-- play Death Grips is Online, Track 1 off Year of the Snitch.
